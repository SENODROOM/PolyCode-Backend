# Q# — Beginner Guide

Follow the numbered folders in order (same layout as the C++ track):

1. `01-Getting-Started` — setup and first quantum programs
2. `02-Basics` — variables, operations, quantum concepts, qubit fundamentals
3. `03-Core-Concepts` — Q# functions and operations
4. `04-Data-Structures` — (as content is added)
5. `05-OOP` — (as content is added)
6. `06-Memory` — internals / deep Q# topics
7. `07-Intermediate` — simulation, optimization, advanced quantum
8. `08-Advanced-Topics` — algorithms, hardware, applications
9. `09-Projects` — hands-on quantum projects
10. `10-Reference` — quick reference and important notes

Start with `01-Getting-Started/01-introduction/`, then `02-Basics/`.
